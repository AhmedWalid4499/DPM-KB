Fortinet Security Fabric

FortiGate NGFWs, FortiSwitch, FortiAP, FortiWLC, FortiManager, and FortiAnalyzer — the fully integrated Security Fabric ecosystem.

ℹ️

FortiOS CLI essentials: Hierarchical config: config system interface / edit port1 / set ip 192.168.1.1/24 / end. Key concept: FortiLink — a special aggregated trunk port connecting a FortiGate to FortiSwitches and FortiAPs, allowing the FortiGate to act as their controller. ASIC acceleration: NP (Network Processor) offloads firewall sessions; CP (Content Processor) handles crypto and inspection. Certification: NSE 1–4 (freely available online) → NSE 7/8 (expert).

FortiGate — Small Branch (40F / 60F / 80F)

NGFW — DESKTOP
FortiGate 40F
Micro-branch and home office NGFW — SD-WAN built in

The FortiGate 40F is the entry point to the FortiGate product line. Despite its small size, it runs full FortiOS — SD-WAN, NGFW, IPS, URL filtering, SSL inspection, and WiFi controller (manages 2 FortiAPs via FortiLink). Two dedicated WAN ports allow dual-ISP failover out of the box. NP6Lite ASIC provides hardware acceleration even at this price point.

FortiGate 60F
Most popular FortiGate model — ideal for small-medium branches up to 50 users

The 60F is the most widely deployed FortiGate model worldwide. It is the "sweet spot" for small to medium branches — 2 WAN ports, 7 LAN ports as an internal switch, and the NP6Lite ASIC delivering 10 Gbps raw firewall throughput. FortiLink enables the 60F to act as a WLC for up to 30 FortiAPs and manage FortiSwitches directly — removing the need for a separate wireless controller in smaller deployments.

NGFW — HIGH END
FortiGate 600E
Large enterprise and data center edge — 10G interfaces throughout

The 600E is for large enterprise perimeters and data center edges requiring 10G connectivity throughout. The NP7 processor delivers 239 Gbps raw firewall throughput with full hardware offload. FortiSwitch

🔗

FortiLink: When a FortiSwitch connects to a FortiGate via FortiLink, the FortiGate becomes the switch controller. You configure VLANs, 802.1X, port policies, and QoS directly from the FortiGate GUI — no need to log into the switch separately. The dedicated FortiLink port on the FortiSwitch is marked with a dashed red border in the diagrams below.

ACCESS SWITCH
FortiSwitch 124E / 124E-POE
24-port desktop access switch — desktop/wall-mount for small offices

The 124E is a compact 24-port switch designed to integrate with a FortiGate via FortiLink. Available in non-PoE (124E) and PoE+ (124E-POE) variants. The POE variant powers IP phones, FortiAPs, and cameras directly. When managed via FortiLink, the FortiGate can dynamically assign VLANs based on user identity (from 802.1X / AD lookup) and apply security policies per-device.

FortiAP — Access Points

ACCESS POINT — WiFi 6
FortiAP 431F
High-density indoor tri-band WiFi 6 AP

The 431F is the high-end indoor FortiAP. Tri-band operation with three concurrent radios provides dedicated backhaul capability — the third radio can act as a wireless uplink to the WLC, freeing the two client radios from having to share backhaul traffic. Managed directly from FortiGate (acting as WLC) over FortiLink, or centrally via FortiManager/FortiCloud.

FortiWLC — Dedicated Wireless Controllers

WLC
FortiWLC 50D / 500D
Dedicated WLC for deployments requiring a separate wireless controller

The FortiWLC is used when a dedicated wireless controller is preferred over using a FortiGate as the controller. This is common in large deployments where the WLC function needs to be separated from the firewall. Supports seamless Layer 3 roaming across subnets — critical for VoIP over WiFi. The 50D manages 50 APs; the 500D scales to 500 APs with a dedicated HA port.

FortiManager & FortiAnalyzer

🖥️

FortiManager

Central policy management for all FortiGate, FortiSwitch, and FortiAP devices. Define policy packages and push to hundreds of devices simultaneously. Uses ADOMs (Administrative Domains) for multi-tenant isolation. Available as hardware (FMG-200G, FMG-1000G) or VM (on VMware, KVM, Azure, AWS).

  • ADOMs — separate environments per customer/tenant
  • Zero-touch provisioning — new FortiGates call home on first boot
  • Policy packages with per-device or per-group overrides
  • FortiSwitch + FortiAP management via FortiLink policies
  • REST API for full automation
📊

FortiAnalyzer

Centralised log aggregation, analytics, and compliance reporting for all FortiGate devices. Correlates events across the Security Fabric to detect threats and provide evidence for compliance audits (GDPR, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001). Available as hardware or VM.

  • Receives syslog and NetFlow from all FortiGate devices
  • Pre-built compliance reports (PCI, HIPAA, GDPR)
  • SIEM-lite: correlation rules and automated alerts
  • Integrates with FortiManager — single console possible
  • Available on Azure and AWS marketplaces
