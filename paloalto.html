Palo Alto Networks

PA-Series NGFWs, Panorama management, Prisma Cloud security, and GlobalProtect VPN. The #1 market-share NGFW vendor.

ℹ️

PAN-OS: All PA firewalls run PAN-OS. Key concepts: Zones (group interfaces), Security Policy (zone-to-zone rules), App-ID (application identification), Commit (changes are in "candidate" config until you commit). Certification path: PCNSA → PCNSE. Management: WebUI (HTTPS), CLI (SSH), or Panorama (centralised).

Wireless (Prisma)

☁️

Palo Alto does not manufacture APs or WLCs. Their wireless play is entirely cloud-based through Prisma Access, which secures users on any WiFi network by tunneling all traffic through a cloud-based NGFW. For AP hardware, customers use Cisco or Fortinet alongside a PA perimeter firewall. See the Wireless page for details on Prisma integration.
