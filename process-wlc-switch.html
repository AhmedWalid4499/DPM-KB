01
Confirm with supply chain that order has arrived at customer site
02
Send customer and confirm with them
03
Confirm with SC that runbook is prepared
04
Set up external dry run with VPO and SC, and customer
05
Set up dates for the staging with the customer
06
Book Field Engineer

07
Raise MACHX for the VPOs
🔧 MACHX
08
Send invitation for Staging
09
Send post-staging email, including daily status (if more than one day)
10
Follow up external dry run with VPO, SC, and customer — check for 3rd Party Servers
11
Send customer the UAT Test document
12
Get confirmation from the customer on the migration date
13
Raise change request on SNOW (Service Now)
❄️ ServiceNow
14
Align any teams needed during migration (DXC, ODC, DHCP Team)
15
Raise MACHX for the VPOs (second raise)
🔧 MACHX
16
Get the answered UAT document from the customer
17
Send migration invitations to customer and VPO (PM and SC as optional)
